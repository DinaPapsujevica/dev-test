AWS Well-Architected

AWS Well-Architected helps engineers create secure, robust, elastic, and powerful infrastructure for any applications and workloads. There are five pillars:

  • security
  • operational excellence
  • reliability
  • performance efficiency
  • cost optimization

AWS Well-Architected provides a solid approach for engineers to assess architectures and implement solutions that can scale over time.

Monitoring systems setup support critical operation and keep processes and procedures manageable and transparent. Key points include automating changes, responding to events, and establishing standards to maintain daily operations.
Building controls to provide security monitoring, track events, and configuration for data confidentiality, user identification, permission management, and system control. Control application functions correctly and consistently. A flexible system automatically recovers from failures to meet business and customer demand. Principal topics include horizontally scalable system architecture, recovery planning, and change handling.
Reasonable usage of computing resources. Selecting the proper resource sizing and scale based on application requirements, monitoring key performance metrics, and making metrics-driven decisions to maintain efficiency as business needs grow.
Reduction of unnecessary cost. Making bills clear and optimizing the provisioned resources, analyzing spends over time, and scaling to meet business needs without extra cost.

What we do

Analyze and assess a client's solution to design and implement the best tradeoff and efficiency between security, reliability, performance, and cost.
